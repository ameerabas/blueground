Blueground (M) Sdn Bhd · Mid-Term Rental Portfolio
Financial Model · Confidential
Base: 23 units, starting balance RM 235,000. Revenue from 21 units = RM 70,000/mo (RM 3,333/unit); 2 newly added units start earning month 1. Revenue ramps toward rent ×1.20 + utilities ×1.30 at maturity. New fixed-rent units earn at 10% markup (months 1–2), 20% (month 3), 30% (month 4+). Base burn RM 119,224 + RM 4,000/mo per new fixed-rent unit + RM 500/mo per new rev-share unit + 5% staffing uplift per 20 new units. For every 10 fixed-rent units added, 3 rev-share units are added. Capex per new fixed-rent unit = deposit + advance + setup = RM 16,750; rev-share = setup only RM 8,000. Cash injections of RM 380,000 then RM 1,000,000 trigger automatically when balance drops below RM 50,000.
